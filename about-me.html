About me

Who I am

I am a UX/UI Design student with a passion for creating simple and intuitive interfaces. Highly team-oriented and eager to gain hands-on experience in the digital field. I discovered this path somewhat by chance, but I immediately realized it was the right one for me. The challenges I've faced along the way have helped me grow, develop my skills, and gain a deeper understanding of myself. I'm driven by the belief that great design has the power to simplify lives, connect people, and leave a lasting impact.
